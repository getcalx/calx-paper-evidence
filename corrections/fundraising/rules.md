## Pipeline
- When scoring investor fit, verify stage claims against actual portfolio behavior. Many funds market as "pre-seed" but gate on traction. The diagnostic: "Walk me through a recent deal where you invested pre-product." No answer = deprioritize for pre-product pitches.
- A prior pass doesn't mean a permanent pass. Pre-seed investors expect companies to evolve. Re-engagement is valid when the story has genuinely changed — but lead with what changed, not with the same pitch.
- Investor update read receipts (Superhuman) are real signal. If a passed investor isn't reading updates, they've fully disengaged — don't waste cycles.
